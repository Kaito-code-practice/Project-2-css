About Us

Welcome to Makboy Resto, your go-to kainan in Rosario, Cavite, where good food meets great friendship. What started as a dream of four friends with a shared passion for cooking has grown into a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike. At Makboy Resto, we believe that every meal should be hearty, flavorful, and made with love—whether it’s our signature tapsi, unli-gravy specials, or home-style comfort food. More than just a place to eat, we’re a place to gather, laugh, and create memories over plates that make you feel at home. Here, it’s not just about feeding the stomach—it’s about feeding the soul. From our friendship to your table, Makboy Resto serves happiness one dish at a time.
